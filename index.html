MECHANICS

Battlefield Systems

💥

Destructible Terrain

Every explosion carves a physically accurate crater into the heightmap. Midpoint Displacement generates a unique landscape each round — no two battles are alike.

🔥

Multi-layer Particles

2,048-particle pool. Additive white flash → orange shockwave → physics debris → rising smoke. All rendered in three blending passes with zero allocations during play.

🎵

Synth + MOD Audio

Cannon shots and explosions synthesised in real-time. Menu and battle each play a different MOD tracker soundtrack via a custom SDL3 AudioStream engine.

🌬️

Wind Physics

Randomised wind changes every turn. It pushes both projectiles and smoke. Factor it in, or aim wide and blame the weather.

🛒

Shop & Economy

Earn credits by landing hits. Spend them between rounds on Nukes, Cluster Bombs, and Penetrators. Strategic shopping wins wars.

📱

Touch + Keyboard

Full gamepad, keyboard and multitouch support. Left half aims, right half sets power, centre tap fires. Works pixel-perfect on any screen size.
